====== Open Bible Stories ======

**50 key stories of the Bible, from Creation to Revelation, for evangelism & discipleship, in text, audio, and video, on any mobile phone, in any language, for free.**

{{  :obs:obs-homepage-web.jpg?nolink&  }}

==== A Free & Open Visual Mini-Bible ====

Open Bible Stories is developed by [[http://distantshoresmedia.org|Distant Shores Media]], [[http://www.wycliffeassociates.org|Wycliffe Associates]], and the Door43 world missions community. To find out more about Open Bible Stories, visit [[https://unfoldingword.org/stories/|openbiblestories.com]].

==== How You Can Help ====

Feel free to join the Open Bible Stories translation team and start a new translation, complete one that has already been started, or help check a completed translation. Learn more about [[:zne:get-started|how you can get started]] or read the [[:zne:obs:help|help section]].
